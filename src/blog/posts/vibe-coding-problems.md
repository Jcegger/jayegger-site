Although LLMs [pre-date](https://toloka.ai/blog/history-of-llms/) the release of ChatGPT, since the release of the chat-bot interface, it has significantly reduced the barrier of entry of software engineering. At least in theory, anyone can launch their own SAAS product with no coding experience. In practice, it's not necessarily that easy. With vibe coding, instead of writing code line-by-line, you write in natural language to delegate creation to an AI assistant. But many of these projects end up having a smorgasbord of issues such as only running on localhost, being riddled with usability bugs, and having security issues.
